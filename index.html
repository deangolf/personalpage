About

Hello, I am Dean Dužaić, Golf Performance Coach, PGA Golf Professional and Golf Sport Developer

A hardworking and motivated golf professional and a postgraduate student with an experience within the very challenging golf market of Croatia. Although equipped with the years of experience within the golf hospitality business, a huge desire for professional learning in area of golf operations and sports coaching led towards furthering education both in the UK and Croatia. Equipped with the strong set of operational and coaching skills is highly committed to developing of golf business at currently the only golf course in Istria region.
